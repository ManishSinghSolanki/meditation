About Us

Through the use of guided meditation and technology, Meditation School has been bringing meditation to thousands of people worldwide since 2002. Our meditation course and guided meditations are based on universal principles that can be discovered in many traditions and practices. They help you to access a universal human ability -- the ability of the mind to be at ease. Our meditation style is compatible with any culture, philosophy, beliefs or lifestyle.
